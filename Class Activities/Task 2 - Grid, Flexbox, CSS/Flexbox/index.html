Caligula

Gaius Caesar Augustus Germanicus also known as Caligula. was Roman emperor from AD 37 until his assassination in AD 41.

Commodus

Caesar Marcus Aurelius Commodus Antoninus Augustus also known as Commodus. was a Roman emperor from 177 to 192 (sole emperor after 180).

Nero

Nero Claudius Caesar Augustus Germanicus also known as Nero. was a Roman emperor and the final emperor of the Julio-Claudian dynasty, reigning from AD 54 until his death in AD 68.
