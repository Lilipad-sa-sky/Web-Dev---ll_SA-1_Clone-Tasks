About Us

At Eun de Chocolat, we believe that chocolate is not just a treat but an experience—a sensory journey that starts with the finest ingredients and ends in sheer delight. Founded by the visionary chocolatier Lilyanne Rousseau, Eun de Chocolat is the culmination of centuries-old techniques blended with modern artistry.

Lilyanne’s passion for chocolate began in her family’s kitchen, where her ancestors, the Rousseau family, had been perfecting their craft for generations. Growing up in a small village nestled in the French countryside, Lilyanne was surrounded by the rich aromas of cacao and caramel. After inheriting the family’s treasured recipes and techniques, she decided to take her love for chocolate to the next level.

Eager to hone her skills, Lilyanne traveled across Europe, learning from the most renowned chocolatiers and patissiers. From the luxurious chocolateries of Paris to the artisan workshops of Switzerland, she immersed herself in the art of chocolate-making. Lilyanne spent years mastering the delicate balance between flavor, texture, and presentation, all while infusing her work with a sense of artistry and innovation.

Her time in Belgium, particularly, was spent working in a prestigious chocolate factory. Where she mastered the precision of large-scale production while maintaining artisanal quality. This experience deepened her understanding of tempering techniques and the art of creating silky, smooth chocolate in every form.

In 1870, after a decade of refining her expertise, Lilyanne returned home to fulfill her lifelong dream of opening Eun de Chocolat. Her boutique quickly gained a reputation for its exquisite attention to detail, blending tradition with modern flair.

From handpicked cacao beans to masterfully designed chocolate creations, every piece at Eun de Chocolat reflects Lilyanne's deep-rooted dedication to quality, tradition, and innovation. Today, our chocolates are enjoyed by connoisseurs worldwide, each bite offering a taste of history , artistry, and pure indulgence.

"Welcome to Eun de Chocolat—where every chocolate has a story, and every story begins with love."

- Lilyanne Rousseau, Founder
