Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Percy Jackson & the Olympians is a fantasy novel series by American author Rick Riordan. The first book series in his Camp Half-Blood Chronicles, the novels are set in a world with the Greek gods in the 21st century.

Currently there are 7 books in the Percy Jackson Series: The Lightning Thief, The Sea of Monsters, The Titan's Curse, The Battle of the Labyrinth, The Last Olympian, The Chalice of the Gods, and The Wrath of the Triple Goddess.
