The 12 Olympians

According to Greek Mythology, they are the 12 major gods who reside in Mt. Olympus. Ruling over Ancient Greece and is tied to many events throughout its existence.

The Olympians consists of Zeus, Poseidon, Demeter, and Hera who are children of the titans gods and goddess Kronos and Rhea. Apollo and Artemis being children of Zeus and the Titan goddess Leto. Hephaestus and Ares being children of Zeus and Hera. Aphrodite born form the essence of Ouranos scattered from the sea. Athena being a daugther of Zeus and the Titan goddess Metis. Hermes son of Zeus and Maia a Pleiades. and Dionysus son of Zeus and the mortal princess Semele.
